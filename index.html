01. About

Hi, I’m Gous. I’m all about creating worlds that feel alive, whether it's a quiet moment or something more intense, I aim to make you experience the scene, not just look at it.

In the world of 3D art, every piece tells a story through textures, lighting, and detail. The goal is always to balance complexity with simplicity, allowing each element to shine in its own way.

I love blending fantasy with reality, adding layers that make you want to explore every corner of the scene. To create something that sticks with you.

I like to take you to places that feel unreal, but still believable. Every scene I create has a bit of chaos, a bit of clarity—just enough to make you want to dive in and explore.
